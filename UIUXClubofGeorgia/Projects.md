## Finding Projects
* Watch YouTube videos on UIUX
* Do not undergo a project you cannot finish
* Ex. redesign a popular application

## Customer Discovery
* Utilize sales, entrepreneurship, and product management skills
* Understand user pain points and needs
* **Observer Bias:** occurs when observers systematically deviate from accurate facts during observation and data recording
    * Avoid observer bias of pain points by conducting field interviews with customers
    * Frame questions in a way so that you maximize learnings of the root problems
    * Try to get a real world understanding of how users interact with an app
* 4-Step Strategy
    * Define Research Goals
    * Define Target Audience
    * Choose Research Methods
    * Create a Research Plan

## Building a Project
* Design solutions to those customer pains
* Genrate MVPs that optimize the current solution to best fit customer needs
* A/B test multiple versions of the app design with customers in follow-up interviews

## Building Your Portfolio
* Do case studies of your work for your portfolio
    * Include challenges, successes, and learnings
    * Include the customer discovery methodology
    * Include info on how you tested the product with real customers
    * Include actionable results, achievements, metrics, and goals reached
    * Good idea is to frame it as a pitch to investors
* Include links to your Figma sketches
