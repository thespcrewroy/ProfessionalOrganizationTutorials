# UIUX Design Theory

## Low-Fidelity Wireframing
* Done on paper
* Done before hi-fidelity design
* Many boxes, circles, and quick sketches
* Simple and early versions of a prototype to highlight function, structure, and process

## Hi-Fidelity Wireframing
* Comprehensive and interactive versions of a prototype
* Generally close to the final product

## User Experience (UX)
* Associated with user's overall experience with a company's product or service

## User Interface (UI)
* How humans interact with a computerized screen or device
